Convention vote

About

The purpose of this page is to facilitate the referendum at Bedford Baptist Church on the matter of whether or the church should join the Convention of Atlantic Baptist Churches.

If you are a member of Bedford Baptist Church, you may and are encouraged to vote in this referendum. Voting ends at 8:00pm ADT, June 13, 2011.

If you have submitted the wrong vote by accident, please email the church office as soon as possible. They will cancel your vote and then you can resubmit it.
