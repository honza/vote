Bedford Baptist Church Convention Vote

About

The purpose of this page is to facilitate the referendum at Bedford Baptist Church on the matter of whether or not the church should join the newly incorporated Convention of Atlantic Baptist Churches.

If you are a member of Bedford Baptist Church, you may and are encouraged to vote in this referendum. Voting starts at 8:00am ADT, June 13, 2011, and ends at 8:00pm the same day.

If you have submitted the wrong vote by accident, please email the church office ( office@bedfordbaptist.ca) as soon as possible. Once you have received confirmation that your vote has been cancelled, you may resubmit your vote.
